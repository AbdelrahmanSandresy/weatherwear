A smart wardrobe assistant that turns weather forecasts into personalized outfit recommendations. Instead of checking the weather and guessing what to wear, users receive tailored clothing suggestions based on temperature, rain and wind conditions.

CRUD: client outfits 
API: https://openweathermap.org/api

App Planning Outline
Fundamental Questions
Identifying the App
What problem does the App solve? - Will facilitate the users ability to choose an outfit based on the weather conditions in their area.

Why would someone use it? - This is built for people who overdress or underdress and end up being too cold, too hot or worse; wet from rain conditions.  

What pain point exists today? - Free outfit picker that saves user time and effort looking up weather and choosing an outfit. reducing decision fatigue. 

How are users solving this now? - manually looking up the weather conditions and dressing accordingly

Who is the User
Who will use this app? - Everyone who wears clothes 

There is not any additional users

What information does this user need?

a. manage a resource b. personal information: email, password, name, etc.

User Goals
What actions do people come here to accomplish? - decide on a weather appropriate outfit

Top 3 reasons for visit?

a. get an outfit recommendation, a quick weather check and add outfits to user's closet

Information Needed
What do users view? - They are likely to view the following resources:

a. profiles b. outfits

What should they never see? - Other users profiles and outfits

What do they create/update/delete? - outfits

User Journey
User should visit my application and be immediately face with a form that will handle BOTH registration and log in. This form should be exchangeable pending on whether the user indicates if they have an existing account or don't.

If a user is registering they should confirm their email and their password prior to a successful registration.

After filling out the form they will either register and/or log in and be sent to the home page where they will see a few paragraphs describing the purpose and intent of this application.

Users may visit a page named closet where all outfits sorted by the date they were created will be on display. Additionally there will be a simple input form for creating a new outfit. If a user click on a trash icon to delete an outfit.

deleting an outfit will always require user confirmation.

User may click on the outfit itself to open a pop up that will show the selected outfit in detail. outfit name, date created, description. Pop up should have an interface with capabilities for saving, deleting an outfit.

A user may visit Contact Us where they will have the ability to reach out for support via email, phone, github, linked in

If something goes wrong a page stating "OOps something went wrong, we have captured this error and will work diligently on fixing it!" with a button named "Home" that will redirect the user back to the home page.

User logs out.
